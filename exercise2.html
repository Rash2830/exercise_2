EWU to remain closed for 2 days after attack 6 august,2018

East West University will remain closed for the next two days, according to a notice of the university. The notice came after police and stick-wielding youths ganged up on the university’s students at its campus in Dhaka’s Rampura today. Due to unavoidable circumstances, the university will remain closed for all students, faculty members, administrative officers and staff on Tuesday, 7 August 2018 and Wednesday, 8 August 2018. The university will reopen on Thursday, 9 August 2018, as usual, says the notice undersigned by Mashfiqur Rahman, acting registrar of the university. The university has also suspended all the examinations and evening classes from August 09 to August 11 2018, according to the notice. However, meetings of Faculty Recruitment Committee (FRC), Academic Council and Non-Teaching Officers Recruitment Committee (NTORC), will be held as per schedule, it added.'
